What to Pack

🛡️

Solo Traveler Tips

Traveling solo in Russia is an incredible experience, but keep these essentials in mind:

  • Internet: Get a local SIM at the airport (Terminal C has MTS/Megafon booths).
  • Money: Exchange cash (USD/EUR) at Komkom Bank for better rates. "Zolotaya Korona" is also an option for transfers.
  • Language: Most people in Murmansk won't speak English; Yandex Translate is your best friend.
  • Documents: Do not lose the Migration Card (small white paper) handed to you at immigration with your passport. Hotels are legally required to see it for your registration.
  • Safety: Share your real-time location with Nomadller support via WhatsApp/Telegram throughout the trip.
